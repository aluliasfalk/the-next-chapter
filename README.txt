VERSION 4.0 INCLUDES
- Installable phone app for iPhone, Android, iPad, and desktop
- In-page “Add to Phone” instructions
- Native install button where supported
- Share button and back-to-top button
- Offline fallback
- Better automatic updating so old app versions are less likely to stay cached
- New “Right Now” keepsake section
- Scroll-reveal transitions
- Improved accessibility and reduced-motion support
- App shortcuts to the countdown and family story
- Continued individual wedding-photo positioning

IPHONE
1. Open the published site in Safari.
2. Tap “Add to Phone” for instructions, or tap Safari’s Share button.
3. Choose “Add to Home Screen.”
4. Tap Add.

ANDROID
Open in Chrome and tap “Add to Phone” or choose “Install app” from the browser menu.

AFTER UPLOADING
Wait 1–2 minutes, refresh the regular website, then close and reopen the home-screen app.
